Biography

Haiyue Zhu is a Scientist with the Adaptive Robotics and Mechatronics Group at A*STAR’s Singapore Institute of Manufacturing Technology (SIMTech). His current research interests include autonomous robotics, computer vision, mechatronics, etc. He received a B.Eng. degree in automation from the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation and a B.Mgt. degree in business administration from the College of Management and Economics, Tianjin University, China. He subsequently received M.Sc. and Ph.D. degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the National University of Singapore.
